# Executive Findings

The synthetic roadmap review identifies 3 initiatives ready for near-term build consideration and 1 initiatives with release gates strong enough for launch review.

- Metric readiness is the main release constraint. 3 of 6 metric contracts are ready without additional event QA.
- Data quality risk is manageable but visible. 4 high-severity quality checks need owner follow-up before launch decisions.
- The highest value PM behavior is to connect roadmap priority, PRD detail, sprint planning, launch gates, and post-launch measurement in one decision packet.

## Recommended Decision

Move the top build-now initiatives into sprint planning only after each has a confirmed metric contract, QA evidence, stakeholder owner, and launch communication plan.
